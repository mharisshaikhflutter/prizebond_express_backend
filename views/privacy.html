Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority.

Information We Collect

PrizeBond does not require personal accounts to use the app. However, we may collect limited technical information:

  • Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • App usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Notification tokens for push messages (via Firebase Messaging).
  • Camera access and text recognition (processed on-device with Google ML Kit).
  • Local preferences and offline storage (via Hive and SharedPreferences).

How We Use Data

The information collected is used to:

  • Improve app performance and reliability.
  • Deliver notifications and updates you opt into.
  • Provide features such as camera-based text recognition and PDF generation.
  • Analyze anonymous usage trends to make the app better.

Security

Sensitive processing, such as camera input and ML Kit text recognition, happens entirely on your device. We do not upload or share your scanned images or files. All communication with third-party services (like Firebase) is encrypted.
